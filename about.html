About Student Music Hub

A passion project born from late-night study sessions and a desperate need for the right music.

Our Story

Started in a Dorm Room

It began during exam season — too distracted by lyrics, too bored by silence, and tired of spending 20 minutes searching for "the right playlist" instead of studying.

Student Music Hub was built to solve exactly that. One place, mood-matched, no ads, no accounts, no friction. Just open and press play.

Share Your Feedback

What We Stand For

Student First

Every design and curation decision is made with one user in mind — the student sitting at 2 AM with a deadline.

Zero Friction

No sign-up. No premium tier. No ads. Open the page and your music is there — exactly as it should be.

Mood-Matched

Science-backed curation. Focus tracks avoid lyrics. Sleep tracks stay below 60 BPM. Every choice is intentional.
